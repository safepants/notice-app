Party Game Questions

The best party games aren't about winning — they're about the moments that happen between rounds. These questions get the whole room involved. Everyone answers, everyone listens, and nobody checks their phone.

  1. 1Everyone point at the funniest person in the room on the count of 3. 1, 2, 3
  2. 2Everyone pick a number from 1 to 100. See who picked closest to each other
  3. 3Show the group your screen time for today
  4. 4Make up a question right now. Everyone has to answer it
  5. 5Describe someone in this room using only three words. Let the group guess who
  6. 6Point at the person who would survive the longest in the wild. Explain
  7. 7If this group started a band, what would it be called?
  8. 8Who's the most intense person here? The group has to agree on one name
  9. 9Without looking — what time do you think it is? Everyone guess. Closest wins
  10. 10Close your eyes. Point to where you think north is. Open. Check. If correct, congratulations — you know North!
  11. 11Everyone be completely silent for 10 seconds. Then describe what you heard
  12. 12If everyone in this room had a secret talent, what would each person's be? Go around and guess
  13. 13Who here isn't seeing your vision right now? Say their name
  14. 14Point at someone. What's their theme song?
  15. 15Assign someone in the room a role in a movie. Describe their character
  16. 16If a stranger watched this group for 5 minutes, what would they assume about you all?
  17. 17Look at everyone's shoes. What do they tell you about the person?
  18. 18Look at everyone for 5 seconds each. Then say who looked the most uncomfortable being looked at
