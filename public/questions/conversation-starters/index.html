frequently asked

How do you start a conversation with someone you don't know?

Ask something specific about the moment you're in. 'Name something beautiful that's within eyesight right now' works because it's about the shared environment, not personal information. Start observational, then go deeper if they're into it.

What's the difference between a conversation starter and an icebreaker?

Icebreakers are designed to get a group talking. Conversation starters are for any context — one-on-one, small groups, even texting. They're less about breaking tension and more about going somewhere interesting.
