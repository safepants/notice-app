frequently asked

How do I get my boyfriend to open up?

Don't ask him to open up — ask him a question worth opening up for. The best way is to go first. Share your own answer honestly, and he'll follow. Questions from a game or a list feel safer than 'we need to talk.'

When should I ask these questions?

When you're already hanging out — not as a scheduled 'relationship check-in.' On a drive, while cooking, or just lying on the couch. The less formal, the more honest the answers.
